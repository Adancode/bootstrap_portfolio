Adam Camacho

I'm a full stack web developer.

My Services

Planning & Design

Using tools like Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Balsamiq, we'll mockup your app before writing a single line of code.

Front End Development

My front end technology stack includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, JSON/API's, Bootstrap, React, and WordPress.

Back End Development

My back end technology stack includes Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, MySQL, PHP, Laravel, and CodeIgniter.

I'm Adam

I'm an entrepreneur that decided to learn to code because the future isn't going to build itself. My coding journey began in mid-2014, and I've now completed over 1,200 hours of self-study, which I've kept track of on blogger. In addition to my self-study, I'm currently attending The Coding Boot Camp at UT Austin, a full stack program which I'll be graduating from in October 2016.

I'm also the founder and organizer of Free Code Camp - Austin, a meetup group whose purpose is to grow Austin's tech community by helping people transition into careers in web development. I have grown the group to over 300 members and membership continues to grow steadily.
